CHEETAH

Cheetah is the fastest Mammal on the earth!.

  • Weight: 40kg - 65kg (88lbs - 140lbs)
  • Top Speed: 112kph (70mph)
  • Life Span: 10 - 12 years
  • Colour: Yellow, Tan, Brown, Black

The Cheetah is a large and powerful feline that was once found throughout Africa and Asia and even in parts of Europe. The Cheetah has a long and slender body that is covered in coarse yellowish fur and dotted with small black spots.
